#Customize Apps

Administrators can add or remove individual application modules in space. Take a "Personal Address Book" as an example.
"Customize Apps" application:

  • In the "Settings" interface, click on the top right corner of the "nine points" of the graphics, will jump out of the "Steedos Apps" pop-up box;
    How to set up "Apps":
  • In the "Admin" interface, click on the "space" under the "Customize Apps";
  • Click on the right side of each app two buttons, respectively, this app can be edited and deleted;
  • Click on the upper right corner of the "new" button, it will jump out "Apps" settings interface;
  • Note: where "name", "link", "icon" is required;
